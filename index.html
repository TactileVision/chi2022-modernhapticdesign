About The Event

About The Event

This workshop aims at gathering people from academia and industry to provide a common ground to discuss various insights on and visions of the field. We wish to collect, compare, contrast and summarize tools used for haptic design. We expect the outcome of the workshop to bea comprehensive overview of existing tools and approaches, along with recommendations on how to move the field forward, together.

Where

Virtual Conference

When

Saturday, 30 April 2022

What are the topics covered in the Workshop ?

The goal of this workshop is two-fold: we want to bring together stakeholders in the field of haptic design with their perspective from an academic or industry stand point to discuss current challenges, and compile an overview of these challenges that would act as recommendations for designing future haptic platforms to avoid reinventing the wheel (once again). We hope to highlight promising areas for haptic design as academia and industry already started doing independently

F.A.Q

  • How to attend the workshop?

    Modern Haptics Design workshop is offered virtually and you can register for the event by reserving your spot during CHI 2022 registration.

  • How to apply as a participant?

    Participants can apply by submitting the google form Call for Participation

  • What should be in the document submitted by the participants?

    Interested participants should write a vision paper formatted as SIGCHI extended abstract (maximum 2 pages long) to introduce their experience in haptic design and their current work in the field. These papers should highlight what kind of experience participants have in the field (industry-centered, academia-centered, mixed, etc) and how this has been impacting their contributions to the field. Ideally, these papers should highlight important challenges participants have been repeatedly facing while designing haptic experiences.

  • Will the submitted paper be published or displayed in the website?

    The submitted paper will be only displayed on the Modern Haptic Design workshop website.

  • Do I need to attend the workshop after submitting an abstract?

    Atleast one participant from a group that submitted an abstract must attend the workshop.

  • What should I do if I am facing problem during submission?

    Please send an email to b3joshi@uwaterloo.ca with the clear details about the issue you are facing.
